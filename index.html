Competitions

Are you ready to unleash your inner innovator, problem solver, and team player? Step into the world of competitive programming that will challenge your skills and ignite your passion for excellence.

Hello World Hackathon

If you're new to coding or taking your first steps in computer science, the "Hello World Competition" is the perfect starting point. Join us in this beginner-friendly event, where you can explore the world of programming, showcase your creativity, and bring your first projects to life. This competition takes place mid-September.

Beginner-Friendly Creative Projects Team Building

CS Games

For those seeking a more intense challenge, the CS Games is for you. It's a thrilling battle of wits, where you'll face off against the sharpest minds from universities across the country. It's not just a competition; it's a chance to represent our institution and measure your skills on a broader stage. Try out in January/February to get on the Concordia teams and compete in March.

Advanced Level Inter-University Prestigious

CyberSci

CyberSci is Canada’s national cybersecurity competition. Compete in regional qualifiers and, if you advance, represent Concordia at the national finals. This event is perfect for students interested in cybersecurity, ethical hacking, and digital forensics, and takes place each mid-November.

Cybersecurity National Team-Based

NorthSec

NorthSec is one of the largest applied security events in North America, featuring a world-class Capture The Flag (CTF) competition. Join the Concordia team to test your skills against international competitors in a high-energy, hands-on environment, every year in May.

Capture The Flag International Security

International Collegiate Contest (ICC)

The ICC is a global programming competition where teams of students solve complex algorithmic problems under time pressure. Represent Concordia and compete with the best from around the world. Tryouts are held in the fall semester.

Algorithms Global Teamwork
